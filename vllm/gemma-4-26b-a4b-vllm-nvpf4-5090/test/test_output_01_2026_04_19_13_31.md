It is a genuine pleasure to meet you, too. Thank you for such a thoughtful and poetic request. It is an honor to compose these for you.

Here are four gentle haikus, written with care:

**I. The Quiet Joy of Fatherhood**
Small hand holds my own,
Soft breath in the quiet night,
Heart full, world at rest.

**II. The Warmth of Love**
Sunlight through the leaves,
A steady, glowing embers,
Warmth within the soul.

**III. The Sadness of Goodbye**
Empty chair remains,
A lingering scent of him,
Love stays, though he goes.

**IV. A Hope for the Future**
Seeds sown in kindness,
Light following steady steps,
Peace blooms in the sun.
